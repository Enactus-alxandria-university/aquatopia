parasite sensor

A high-tech guardian against aquatic pests. With facial recognition precision, it swiftly spots isopods, protecting fish stocks and infrastructure. It's like having a vigilant sentry for your underwater world, ensuring a thriving aquatic environment.



Cellulose Conversion

Our cellulose conversion innovation transforms cellulose sourced from common reeds into high-quality silica using state-of-the-art technology.

This silica serves as a versatile material for various eco-friendly applications, ranging from sustainable packaging to advanced textiles and green construction materials. It epitomizes our commitment to harnessing nature's resources responsibly, offering innovative solutions for a more sustainable future.




Organic Fertilizers

Our isopod-derived organic fertilizers offer a sustainable solution for soil enrichment. Through a meticulous process, isopods are transformed into nutrient-rich fertilizers, providing essential elements for plant growth while promoting soil health.

This innovative approach not only addresses agricultural needs but also contributes to environmental sustainability by repurposing aquatic pests into valuable resources for farming practices.



Chitin Extraction

transforming isopod shells into nature's versatile wonder material. From water purification to wound healing, it's nature's gift repurposed for a sustainable future.
